Don't Starve is a survival game first launched back in April 2013, developed and maintained by Klei Entertainment. You can find it on Steam.

As the name of the game suggests, the main goal of the game is to "not starve" (or in other words, survive).
There is a wide variety of ways a player can die in. They can get eaten by dogs.

They can get stomped by Deerclops and reduce their sanity to crumbles.

Or they can be caught by nightfall and simply dissapear in the middle of the night.

While there is plenty of dangers to avoid, there are also plenty of ways to survive. Players can collect all sorts of stuff and materials like wood, grass, rocks, flints, twigs, gold and use them to create all sorts of more advance survival tools. For example, you can make an axe using 2 flints and 1 twig, which you can then use to cut down trees and collect wood to make a fire pit.

To create even more advanced stuff(weapons, clothing, shelter, more advanced building materials and many more) you can build a science machine that will grand the player with the needed ideas.

While the "classic" Don't Starve was the first flavor of the game, over the years, Klei Entertainment have improved and released many additional content expansions of Don't Starve. The franchise includes: Don't Starve, Don't Starve Reign Of Giants, Don't Starve Shipwrecked, Don't Starve Together and Don't Starve Hamlet.
